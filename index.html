Welcome to my portfolio


As a recently graduated games programming student, I am eagerly seeking to make my mark in the gaming industry. My primary areas of expertise lie in C# and Unity, but I also have some experience with Unreal and C++. Throughout my educational journey and personal pursuits, I have completed multiple projects, each serving as a testament to my passion and growth as a programmer. This portfolio serves as a showcase for my work, highlighting the diverse range of projects I have developed.

BYLE

BYLE is the most intricate game I've worked on. With plans to soon go on Steam and having been presented at the Insomnia gaming festival, BYLE is something I am very proud of. I worked on it in a group, and my major roles in development were procedural generation, the shop systems, the tutorial, the encyclopedia, room management, various shaders and sound implementation. Additionally, I collaborated with a teammate to implement the enemies into the game using AI and also designed the models and animations for the majority of the game's enemies. I was also involved in the player controller, game manager, and weapons. I learned a lot while developing BYLE and see it as the culmination of all my work to this point.
